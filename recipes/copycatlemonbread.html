Copycat of Starbucks Lemon Bread

A recipe taken from AllRecipes which is a copycat of Starbucks Lemon Bread.

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 2 (2.9 ounce) packages non-instant lemon pudding mix
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 8 ounces sour cream
  • ½ cup milk
  • 6 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice

Icing:

  • 2 ½ cups confectioners' sugar
  • 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice, or more to taste
